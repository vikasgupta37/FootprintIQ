## Demo Flow (10 minutes)

### 1. Introduction (1 minute)

**Opening:**
> "Hi everyone! I'm excited to present **FootprintIQ** - an AI-powered platform that transforms sustainability awareness into action.
>
> The problem? Most people contribute significantly to carbon emissions without understanding their environmental impact. Current tools are complex, generic, and lack motivation.
>
> FootprintIQ changes this with personalized AI coaching, predictive modeling, and our flagship innovation - the **Eco Twin**."

**Slide: Problem Statement**
- 78% can't estimate their carbon footprint
- Existing tools lack personalization
- Low engagement and retention

---

### 2. Carbon Calculator Demo (2 minutes)

**Navigate to Calculator**

> "Let me show you how easy it is to understand your impact. I'll quickly input my lifestyle data..."

**Live Demo:**
1. Transportation: "I drive a petrol car, 600km/month, rarely use public transport, took 2 short-haul flights"
2. Energy: "350 kWh/month, 20% renewable, moderate AC usage"
3. Food: "Occasional meat eater, medium dairy"
4. Shopping: "2 clothing items/month, 8 online deliveries"
5. Waste: "Often recycle, use reusable water bottle"

**Click Calculate** (show loading animation)

**Results appear:**
> "Within 2 seconds, FootprintIQ calculates my complete carbon footprint: **4.64 tons CO2e annually** - that's a GOOD grade, but there's room for improvement.
>
> Notice how it breaks down by category - transportation is my biggest contributor at 42%. The platform immediately shows comparisons to national and global averages."

**Highlight:**
- Visual breakdown pie chart
- Grade indicator with color
- Comparison metrics

### 3. AI Sustainability Advisor (2 minutes)

**Click on AI Advisor tab**

> "Now here's where it gets intelligent. Meet your personal sustainability coach powered by Claude Opus 4.5."

**Type:** "How can I reduce my transportation emissions?"

**AI Response streams in real-time:**
> (Show response appearing token-by-token)
>
> "Watch how it personalizes the response based on my actual data - it knows my 30km commute and suggests specific actions ranked by impact and feasibility."

**AI suggests:**
- 🚌 Public transport 2 days/week: 340 kg saved, saves $1,800/year, Easy
- 🚗 Carpool 3 days/week: 510 kg saved, Medium difficulty
- ⚡ Electric vehicle: 1,560 kg saved, High impact, Long-term

**Highlight:**
- Context-aware recommendations
- Real data integration
- Actionable steps with impact quantification
- Streaming response (real-time)

**Follow-up question:**
> "Let me ask something educational..."

**Type:** "Why is beef bad for the environment?"

**Show AI response with citations:**
> "Notice how it cites sources - IPCC 2023, FAO 2024 - and even personalizes the advice based on my occasional meat-eating status."

> - Payback period: 18.75 years
> - Feasibility score: 68/100 (considers my profile)
>
> This helps you make informed decisions with complete transparency."

**Show comparison chart:**
- Side-by-side before/after

- Timeline visualization

## Q&A Preparation

**Expected Questions:**

**Q:** "How accurate are the carbon calculations?"  
**A:** "We use IPCC-validated emission factors and regularly update our database. Our calculations are peer-reviewed and cite sources transparently."

**Q:** "What makes Eco Twin different?"  
**A:** "Unlike static calculators, Eco Twin uses AI to model complex scenarios with financial analysis, feasibility scoring, and personalized recommendations. It's predictive, not just descriptive."

**Q:** "How do you monetize?"  
**A:** "Freemium model: free calculator and basic AI advice. Premium ($9.99/month) adds unlimited AI, advanced analytics, priority support. B2B SaaS for corporations ($99/seat/month)."

**Q:** "What about data privacy?"  
**A:** "GDPR compliant, encrypted data, users own their data, easy export/deletion. We never sell user data."

**Q:** "How scalable is this?"  
**A:** "Built on AWS with auto-scaling ECS, can handle 100K+ concurrent users. We've load-tested to 50K users with sub-200ms API response times."

**Q:** "AI costs?"  
**A:** "We optimize through prompt caching, streaming, and smart rate limiting. Average cost: $0.03/user/month for AI. With 200K users, that's $6K/month, manageable with our $50K MRR projection at month 6."

## Presentation Tips

1. **Energy:** Be enthusiastic but natural
2. **Pacing:** Speak clearly, not too fast
3. **Engagement:** Make eye contact with judges
4. **Demo:** Let the product shine, minimize talking during demos
5. **Backup:** Have video recording ready if live demo fails
6. **Time:** Practice to stay within 10 minutes
7. **Confidence:** You built something amazing, show it!
